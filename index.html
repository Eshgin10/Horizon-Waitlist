Frequently Asked Questions

When will Horizon be available?

Horizon is scheduled for release on December 31, 2025. Early access will be granted to waitlist members starting in October 2025. Join our waitlist to be among the first to experience it!

What platforms will Horizon support?

Horizon will be available on macOS, Windows, iOS, Android, and as a web application. Our cross-platform support ensures you can work seamlessly across all your devices.

Is there a free tier available?

Yes! Horizon will offer a generous free tier with core functionality for individuals and small teams. Premium features will be available through our Pro and Enterprise plans.

How does Horizon handle data privacy?

We take privacy seriously. All data is encrypted end-to-end, stored in secure data centers, and never shared with third parties. We're fully GDPR and CCPA compliant, and you always maintain ownership of your data.
